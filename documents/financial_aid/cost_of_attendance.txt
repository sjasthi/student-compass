Cost of Attendance Overview

The cost of attendance represents an estimate of the total expenses for a full academic year. It includes more than tuition and fees. These estimates help determine financial aid eligibility.

Typical cost categories:
- Tuition and mandatory fees
- Books and course materials
- Housing and food
- Transportation
- Personal expenses
- Loan fees (if applicable)

Actual costs vary depending on enrollment level, living situation, and individual choices.
