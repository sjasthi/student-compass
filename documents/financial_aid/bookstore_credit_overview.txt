Bookstore Credit Program Overview

The bookstore credit program allows eligible students to charge textbooks and required course materials to their student account before financial aid is disbursed.

Key points:
- Available for a limited period at the start of each term
- Can be used for textbooks and required supplies
- Charges are applied to the student account and covered by financial aid once disbursed
- Students are responsible for any remaining balance not covered by aid

The program opens and closes on specific dates each term.
