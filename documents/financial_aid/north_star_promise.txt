North Star Promise Scholarship Overview

The North Star Promise Scholarship is a Minnesota program designed to make college more affordable for eligible residents. It is a “last-dollar” scholarship, meaning it covers remaining tuition and fees after other federal and state grants have been applied.

General features:
- Available to Minnesota residents who meet income and eligibility requirements
- Applies to undergraduate programs at eligible institutions
- Covers remaining tuition and fees after grants and scholarships
- Requires students to complete the financial aid application process

Eligibility and award amounts may vary based on state guidelines.
