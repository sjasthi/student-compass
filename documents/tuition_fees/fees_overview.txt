Fees Overview

Metro State charges several fees in addition to tuition. These fees apply to all courses taken for credit, including online courses. All fees are subject to change.

Per-Credit Fees
- Activity Fee: $4 per credit.
- Student Center Fee: $8.50 per credit. Supports the construction, maintenance, and operation of the student center.
- Technology Fee: $14 per credit. Supports student computer labs and instructional technology.
- MSUSA Fee: $0.80 per credit. Supports statewide student representation and activities.
- Healthcare Fee: $2 per credit. Helps provide access to contracted health services.
- Parking Fee: $13 per credit. Supports construction, maintenance, and operation of campus parking facilities.

Other Fees
- Graduate application fee: $20
- Doctoral application fee: $40
- Graduation fee: $20
- Returned payment fee: $20
- Tuition late fee: $30
- Transcript fees: $8 standard, $13 rush, $5 online
- Senior citizen tuition rate: $20 per credit
- Additional course and lab fees: varies by course
- Noncredit workshop fees: varies
- Payment plan fees: varies
- Replacement ID fees: varies
- Health insurance fees: varies
