Course Delivery Methods Overview

Metro State offers courses in several delivery formats. Students should review the delivery method before registering to ensure the format fits their schedule and learning preferences.

In-Person
Courses meet on campus at scheduled days and times. Attendance is required.

Online Asynchronous
Coursework is completed online without scheduled meeting times. Students work independently but must meet deadlines.

Online Synchronous
Courses meet online at scheduled times using video conferencing. Attendance is required during meeting times.

Hybrid
Courses combine in-person meetings with online coursework. The schedule specifies which sessions are on campus and which are online.

HyFlex
Students may choose to attend each class session in person, online synchronously, or online asynchronously, depending on their preference.

Web-Enhanced
Courses meet in person but use online tools for assignments, discussions, or supplemental materials.

Independent Study
Students work one-on-one with a faculty member to complete coursework individually. Requires approval.

Internship/Practicum
Learning occurs at an off-campus site under supervision. Requirements vary by program.

Students should review course notes in the schedule for additional details about expectations and technology requirements.
