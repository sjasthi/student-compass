First-Year Admissions Overview

Students apply as first-year applicants if they are currently in high school or have completed fewer than 16 transferable college-level semester credits.

Admission Categories
1. Automatic Admission
   - Requires a cumulative high school GPA of 2.75 or higher
   - GED applicants may qualify with test scores of 165 or higher on each subject
   - Some academic programs may require higher GPAs

2. Conditional Admission
   - For students with a cumulative GPA between 2.5 and 2.75
   - Students receive additional academic support and must meet specific requirements before and during their first semester

3. Community College Transfer Pathway
   - For students with a cumulative GPA below 2.5
   - Students must attend a community college before reapplying as transfer students
   - Minnesota State community college pathways allow seamless transfer of credits

Transcript Requirements
Applicants must submit:
- Official high school transcript
- Official transcripts from any post-secondary institutions attended
- Official AP test scores (if applicable)

Additional Notes
- Early application is encouraged to allow time for transfer credit evaluation (DARS), course planning, financial aid packaging, and orientation.
- Admission decisions may include conditions that must be met before registering for courses.
- Applicants can check their application status and confirm receipt of transcripts through the eServices applicant portal.
