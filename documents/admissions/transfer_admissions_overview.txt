Transfer Admissions Overview

Students who have attempted at least 16 transferable, college-level semester credits at another institution apply to Metro State as transfer students. Transferable credits must be non-developmental and from an accredited post-secondary institution.

Admission Categories
1. Automatic Admission
   - Requires a cumulative GPA of 2.0 or higher
   - At least 16 transferable semester credits attempted
   - Some academic programs may require a higher GPA

2. Conditional Admission
   - For students with a cumulative GPA between 1.5 and 2.0
   - Students receive additional academic support and must meet specific requirements during their first semester

3. Community College Transfer Pathway
   - For students with a cumulative GPA below 1.5
   - Students must attend a community college to improve their GPA before reapplying
   - Minnesota State community college pathways allow seamless transfer of credits

Transcript Requirements
Transfer applicants must submit official transcripts from all post-secondary institutions attended, including:
- Colleges or universities where credits were attempted
- Military credits
- CLEP or AP credits

Additional Notes
- Early application is encouraged to allow time for transfer credit evaluation (DARS), course planning, financial aid packaging, and orientation.
- Admission decisions may include conditions that must be met before registering for courses.
- Applicants can check their application status and confirm receipt of transcripts through the eServices applicant portal.
